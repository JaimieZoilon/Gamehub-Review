Wuthering Waves

Wuthering Waves is a story-rich open-world action RPG with a high degree of freedom from Kuro Game.

★ ★ ★ ★ ★
Gameplay: "Wuthering Waves' combo-heavy action, mobility, and monster taming enhance combat and exploration enough to respectably distinguish it from competitors." — IGN
Graphics: "Wuthering Waves is a fairly good-looking game. It won’t really wow you but it definitely isn’t that bad." — PlayForge
Sound: "I think I’m more impressed with the audio than the graphics. Especially when it comes to some of the voice lines of the characters." — PlayForge

Cyberpunk 2077

Cyberpunk 2077 is an open-world, action-adventure story set in Night City, a megalopolis obsessed with power, glamour, and body modification.

★ ★ ★ ★ ★
Gameplay: "Cyberpunk 2077 throws you into a beautiful, dense cityscape and offers a staggering amount of flexibility in how you choose to take it from there." — IGN
Graphics: "Cyberpunk 2077 in 2024, what's it like? Undoubtedly you've heard about the terrible launch and all the game breaking bugs, particularly on last-gen consoles. But after significant updates, the graphics look stunning!" — TechRadar
Sound: "The sound design truly brings Night City to life with its atmospheric sounds, intense combat effects, and a brilliant soundtrack." — PC Gamer

Assassin's Creed Shadow

Assassin's Creed is a third-person stealth/action game franchise, and recently, a trans-media franchise. Mechanically, it's most known for offering total freedom of movement and the ability to climb or do parkour on anything you see.

★ ★ ★ ★ ★
Gameplay: "The series' core of stealth and parkour is as strong as ever, but the new mechanics offer a fresh challenge." — GameSpot
Graphics: "The graphics are truly breathtaking with detailed environments, character models, and smooth animations." — IGN
Sound: "The sound design gives life to the action with dramatic music and ambient noises, enhancing the immersive experience." — Eurogamer
